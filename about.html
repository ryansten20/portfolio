About

I’m Ryan, a developer who enjoys bringing creative ideas to life through code. My interests span game development, web design, and embedded systems, and I like exploring how technology can be used in fun, interactive, and practical ways.

Some of my favorite projects include Duck Quest, a Unity-based 2D adventure game, and a custom compiler for the bC language written in C++ with Flex and Bison. I’m also building DMX-controlled LED glasses that sync with stage lighting systems—a mix of hardware, microcontrollers, and creative design. Each project has pushed me to learn new tools, problem-solve in different ways, and grow as a developer.

Outside of computer science, I enjoy collecting Pokémon cards, playing games, and working on personal side projects. I’m also into cars, sports such as UFC and football, playing pool and most importantly, I like to keep learning, building, and staying active.
